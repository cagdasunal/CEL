#### What happens during your first week at an english language school?
Starting at a new english language school can feel intimidating at first. Do not worry. Every Monday, fresh classes begin with a clear orientation. You take a placement test to determine your exact baseline. This ensures you sit next to peers at the same proficiency stage. The curriculum moves quickly. Within 48 hours, you will join your assigned group and start participating in conversation-driven lessons. You immediately practice structures you can use outside the building. The neighborhood around Coal Harbour provides the perfect testing ground. You will buy lunch at Granville Island or grab coffee in Gastown. These daily interactions solidify what you review in the classroom. Need extra help? Teachers monitor your weekly assessments to catch any struggles early. They know your name. They know your goals.

Choosing the right curriculum depends entirely on what you want to achieve. Many students start with a standard foundation at our english language school. You simulate business meetings, analyze artificial intelligence trends, and practice project management vocabulary. This builds immediate career-ready skills. Not sure which track fits best? Take a moment to [explore all of our classes](https://www.englishcollege.com/courses) and compare the weekly schedules.

#### Where will you live while attending an english language school?
Housing changes your entire study abroad experience at an english language school. You have distinct choices based on your budget and desired independence. Many learners opt for a homestay to guarantee daily conversation practice. Living with local hosts forces you to use your new vocabulary at the dinner table. It provides a built-in support system. Other students prefer the autonomy of shared apartments. This option connects you with international peers who are also navigating life in a new country. You cook together, explore Stanley Park together, and practice together. Both paths have distinct advantages. You can review the details on our [housing accommodation page](https://www.englishcollege.com/housing) to see which setup aligns with your comfort level. The proximity of your housing to the downtown core dictates how you spend your mornings. Most locations offer easy transit commutes.

#### Why do small classes at an english language school accelerate fluency?
The math is simple. Fewer students mean more speaking time. When you attend an english language school that caps enrollment at 12 people per room, you cannot hide in the back row. You must speak. You must answer questions. This constant engagement builds confidence rapidly. The Commission on English Language Program Accreditation (CEA) recognizes facilities across the industry that maintain high academic standards, and rigorous teacher feedback is a core part of that success. Your instructors catch your recurring mistakes immediately. They correct your pronunciation before it becomes a habit. This is why our students consistently report clear, measurable improvement week over week. They leave ready to navigate the world.
