Coastal Education

<!-- #summary-title (plain text) -->
Why choose a language school in San Diego?

<!-- #summary-paragraph (plain text) -->
CEL operates an accredited language school located steps from the ocean in Pacific Beach, San Diego, California. With an average of just seven students per class and 14 modern classrooms equipped with interactive whiteboards, students receive highly focused instruction. Choosing to study English here means combining rigorous academic preparation with the California coastal lifestyle. You can spend your mornings building essential grammar and speaking skills, then walk down Garnet Avenue to practice real-world communication. The campus sits close to Mission Bay and Crystal Pier, providing a relaxing environment that accelerates learning. Students often find that immersion in this supportive community makes mastering the English language feel natural. By keeping class sizes small, teachers can personalize lessons to fit individual goals, whether you are preparing for university admission or simply looking to improve everyday conversational fluency.

#### What specific programs does the language school offer?
Beyond standard General English classes, students can select specialized electives tailored to their goals. If you plan to attend a university in the United States, you might enroll in preparation classes for the Test of English as a Foreign Language internet-Based Test (TOEFL iBT). We also offer Academic Skills and Global Pathway programs for college readiness. Students looking for a unique physical activity can choose our English Plus Surfing option, which includes four hours of surf instruction per week right at local breaks. Whether you want to improve basic conversation skills or secure a high exam score, [our courses](https://www.englishcollege.com/courses) adapt to your specific needs.

#### How much does it cost to study here?
Pricing depends on your course duration and intensity. It varies. We frequently run special promotions for our San Diego campus. For example, a recent offer provided a 35 percent discount on our tuition fees, bringing the cost of a 12-week program down to $3,078 instead of $4,590. These tuition adjustments make long-term study more accessible. When you calculate your total fees, remember to account for accommodation and living expenses in Southern California. Check our current listings to find the most up-to-date pricing and secure a favorable rate for your studies.

#### What are the accommodation options nearby?
Many students choose the CEL Shared Apartment Superior, located right in Pacific Beach. This complex features a pool, jacuzzi, and barbecue area. Living with other international students provides continuous language practice outside the classroom. You can easily commute to the language school, walk to the beach, and build friendships that last long after your course ends. If you prefer a different living arrangement, our coordinators can help you find suitable [housing options](https://www.englishcollege.com/housing) across the city.

#### Can you transfer to other locations?
CEL operates multiple campuses across North America. If you want to experience different cities during your time abroad, you can split your studies. After spending a few months in San Diego, you might decide to [learn more about the Los Angeles campus](https://www.englishcollege.com/los-angeles-ca/language-courses) or head north to study in Vancouver. The Commission on English Language Program Accreditation (CEA) sets rigorous standards for our US locations, ensuring a consistent educational experience regardless of which city you choose to call home.
