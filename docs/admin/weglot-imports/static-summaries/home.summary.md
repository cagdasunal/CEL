#### How do the campus locations differ?
Each destination has a distinct character that shapes the daily experience. Our [CEL San Diego campus](https://www.englishcollege.com/san-diego-ca/language-school) sits in the Pacific Beach neighborhood just three blocks from the ocean. You get year-round Southern California weather and a regular schedule of outdoor community events. Alternatively, you can study at our [CEL Los Angeles language center](https://www.englishcollege.com/los-angeles-ca/language-courses), which operates from the Santa Monica beachfront business district. This puts you within walking distance of the entertainment, technology, and creative industries that define the region. Our third option is downtown Vancouver near Coal Harbour. It ranks among the world's most livable cities and stands as the only Canadian destination in our network. No matter which city you select, the core english language school curriculum remains consistent.

#### What programs does the english language school offer?
Programs run from one-week intensives through multi-month progressions toward advanced fluency. Beginners focus on foundational vocabulary and grammar structures. Advanced learners tackle complex academic writing or professional communication. Both groups progress quickly. If you want to study at a North American university, we provide a dedicated [pathway program in the USA](https://www.englishcollege.com/pathway-program-usa) to help you transition into higher education without taking the TOEFL iBT exam. We also offer specialized test preparation. You can take exam-focused classes for Cambridge English qualifications. Every student takes a placement test on their first day at our english language school. This ensures you enter a class that matches your exact skill level. The Common European Framework of Reference (CEFR) guides our level progression from A1 through C1. Your teachers assess your progress regularly, ensuring you advance to the next level as soon as you demonstrate mastery of the current material.

#### How long does it take to learn English fluently?
Progress depends on your starting level, study habits, and course intensity. The timeline varies. Most students who attend our english language school reach a working B2 level in twelve weeks of full-time study. This timeline assumes 25 hours of class per week plus an hour of homework most evenings. If you skip homework, expect the process to take longer. Immersion accelerates learning. Spending time with international friends forces you to use the target language outside of school hours. Living with a local host family or sharing an apartment with classmates provides constant practice opportunities. Curious about timelines in Canada? You can review [how long it takes to learn English in Vancouver](https://www.englishcollege.com/vancouver/how-long-to-learn-english) for specific duration estimates based on intensive scheduling. Every student learns at a different pace.

#### What are the housing arrangements for international students?
Finding a comfortable place to live is critical for your success at an english language school. We organize several safe, convenient [student housing options](https://www.englishcollege.com/housing) near our campuses. Homestays offer complete cultural immersion. You live with a local family, eat meals together, and practice conversational skills in a natural home environment. For those seeking more independence, shared student apartments provide excellent amenities. You might cook dinner with a classmate from Brazil, study in a common lounge, or relax by the pool. Living with other international students creates a supportive community. It makes the transition to a new country much easier.

Immersion in a native-speaking country forces you to adapt quickly. It works. You order coffee, navigate public transit, and ask for directions in the local language every single day. You learn to communicate across cultural boundaries. You build a worldwide network of friends. Whether you want to improve your career prospects, prepare for university, or simply enjoy a great cultural exchange, mastering the language opens doors.
