How does our language school in Los Angeles work?

<!-- #summary-paragraph (plain text) -->
Choosing our language school in Los Angeles places you exactly 6 blocks from the Santa Monica beach in California. The campus, now operated by EC Los Angeles, features 13 modern classrooms equipped with smart TVs in a beautiful building right on Wilshire Boulevard. Students join classes averaging 11 learners, maxing out at 15, to ensure personalized attention from certified teachers. You can start a general english program any Monday, with instruction catering to absolute beginners through advanced levels. Our facility holds Commission on English Language Program Accreditation (CEA) credentials, ensuring your education meets strict industry standards. Most students notice a rapid improvement in their daily confidence simply by using their new skills at cafes along the nearby Third Street Promenade right after class. Standard tuition fees cover registration and learning materials, while many international students secure a significant discount on long-term bookings to make their coastal study experience more affordable.

#### How do small class sizes improve fluency?
When you study in southern California, you want enough personal speaking time to actually improve. Classes at our Santa Monica campus average just 11 students. This small ratio allows instructors to correct your pronunciation in real time and gives you ample opportunity to practice speaking. Whether you are taking private lessons or joining group sessions, you will not get lost in the back of a crowded room. Teachers focus on practical communication that you can immediately take outside and test at the Santa Monica Pier.

#### What should you know about tuition and pricing?
Understanding the cost of your education helps you plan a better trip. The exact fees depend on how long you intend to stay and which intensity level you select. We update pricing regularly to reflect current availability, and students can often apply a promotional discount to their total cost. These limited-time offers apply directly to your [general English courses](https://www.englishcollege.com/courses/general-english) and usually bundle the initial registration and necessary learning materials into one predictable price. Shorter stays of 4 weeks are available, but students booking 12 weeks or more tend to see the largest reductions in their weekly rate.

#### How does the location enhance your studies?
Living the true California lifestyle accelerates language acquisition because you are constantly engaged with native speakers. After your morning sessions wrap up, the coastal environment becomes your secondary classroom. Students frequently rent bicycles to ride along the beachfront or organize afternoon volleyball games near the water. If you want to [learn English in the USA](https://www.englishcollege.com/learn-english-usa), positioning yourself near major cultural landmarks like Hollywood and Venice Beach provides endless topics for conversation practice. The artistic energy of the city encourages international learners to step out of their comfort zones, share experiences with locals, and build a truly global mindset.

#### Can beginners apply to this campus?
Yes. We welcome learners at every proficiency level across our network, whether you study here, in San Diego, California, or up north in Vancouver, British Columbia. New intake periods open every Monday at the campus, making it easy to align your start date with your travel schedule. Absolute beginners receive foundational vocabulary and grammar instruction, while advanced speakers tackle complex idioms and professional communication styles. You simply take a placement test before arriving. This step ensures you sit in a room with peers who share your exact level, keeping the curriculum challenging but never overwhelming.
