How long does it take to learn english in the USA?

Choosing to learn english in the USA with the College of English Language (CEL) means studying at one of three California campuses run by a Student and Exchange Visitor Program (SEVP) certified school. Our programs are accredited by the Commission on English Language Program Accreditation (CEA). For decades, we have welcomed international students aged 16 and older to master a new language. We operate four locations across our network, including three specific campuses in San Diego, California, and Los Angeles, California. Most students aiming to progress through the Common European Framework of Reference (CEFR) levels from A1 to C2 require about 12 weeks of full-time immersion per level. Small class sizes average 7 students per group. This setup ensures you receive direct feedback from certified instructors. You progress quickly. New courses start every Monday throughout the year. You practice daily in an authentic American cultural setting. Every year, we host over 1,000 students from more than 23 nationalities.

While studying in the United States, your daily environment shapes your progression. Students who engage in local activities and live with native speakers consistently reach their language goals faster. Whether you choose the relaxed coastal setting of the Pacific Ocean or the entertainment center of Santa Monica, daily interactions at cafes, markets, and social events cement your classroom knowledge. We also operate a fourth campus in Vancouver, British Columbia, for students considering a Canadian experience. You will experience nuanced accents, regional dialects, and everyday expressions that textbooks simply cannot capture.

Cost is a major factor for international students planning an extended stay. We regularly update our pricing to provide a competitive discount on tuition for extended enrollments. A 12-week intensive track often qualifies for reduced fees, saving you hundreds of dollars compared to short-term bookings. We occasionally waive the registration fee during specific periods, making your initial investment highly manageable. Securing an early offer allows you to budget accurately for flights, local transport, and daily living expenses across Southern California.

#### What makes San Diego and Los Angeles ideal study locations?

The two USA cities feel very different day-to-day. San Diego sits on the coast with year-round mild weather averaging 23°C. The beach culture stretches from Pacific Beach to La Jolla. You have easy weekend access across the border to Tijuana, Mexico. We operate two distinct centers here. San Diego Downtown puts you in the business district, while San Diego Pacific Beach is steps from the sand. Santa Monica sits just west of Los Angeles proper. This location puts students within walking distance of the entertainment, technology, and creative industries that define the region. The ocean is just three blocks from your classroom. You can explore [our English courses in Los Angeles](https://www.englishcollege.com/los-angeles-ca/language-courses) to see how the local environment enhances academic focus. Both cities provide exceptional outdoor recreation. You might hike the Santa Monica Mountains or camp in Joshua Tree National Park over a long weekend.

#### How do you choose the right course level and duration?

Our program covers 9 distinct courses tailored to specific goals. You take a placement test upon arrival to determine your exact starting point on the CEFR scale. Our [general English programs](https://www.englishcollege.com/courses/general-english) focus on everyday speaking, listening, reading, and writing skills. Academic tracks prepare you for university lectures and rigorous testing. If your goal is higher education, [our USA pathway programs](https://www.englishcollege.com/pathway-program-usa) provide a structured bridge to partner institutions. You gain direct entry to local colleges without needing a separate TOEFL or IELTS score. Certified instructors track your progress weekly. They identify areas requiring extra attention and adjust your study plan accordingly. We adjust. You reach your specific academic targets on a predictable timeline.

#### What are the costs and available discounts for California programs?

Studying abroad is a major financial commitment. We frequently run special promotions to lower the barrier to entry for international learners. A typical 12-week enrollment might drop from $4,590 to $3,294 when you secure a seasonal discount. These promotional rates often include your learning materials and the initial registration fee. Checking the latest promotional offer ensures you receive the best possible value for your time abroad. Managing your budget properly allows you to experience more of California outside the classroom. Review our guide on [budgeting for a California student stay](https://www.englishcollege.com/post/california-student-budget-learn-english) to understand everyday expenses like transportation, food, and weekend travel. Preparation pays off.

#### Where do international students live during their studies?

Accommodation directly impacts your comfort and academic success. Choices matter. We coordinate 19 different housing options across our USA locations. Homestays with local American families provide the highest level of cultural immersion. You share daily meals, participate in family routines, and practice conversational skills constantly. Shared apartments offer more independence for mature students. These residences place you alongside peers from around the world. You manage your own meals and daily schedule while building an international network. Review [our student housing choices](https://www.englishcollege.com/housing) early in your application process, as premium spots fill quickly before the busy summer months.

#### Are there special programs for younger learners?

We offer dedicated options for high school students who want to experience American culture during their school breaks. These structured packages combine morning classroom instruction with afternoon activities. Students visit local landmarks, try surfing, and build friendships with international peers. The [San Diego summer camp](https://www.englishcollege.com/summer-camp-san-diego) provides a safe, highly supervised environment for teenagers. They gain confidence in their speaking abilities while exploring famous Southern California attractions under the guidance of experienced staff. The schedule is packed.

#### How does language study lead to career opportunities?

Mastering a second language opens doors in the global job market. Southern California is a hub for technology, business, and entertainment. Your time here helps you build an international network that stands out on your CV. You learn the specific vocabulary needed to navigate corporate meetings, draft professional emails, and present ideas confidently. Read more about preparing for international business on the [CEL global blog](https://www.englishcollege.com/blog). Gaining fluency gives you a distinct advantage whether you plan to return home or pursue further opportunities abroad. Hard work works.
