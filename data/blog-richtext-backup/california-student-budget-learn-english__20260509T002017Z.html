Why San Diego Is the Smartest Place to Learn English in California

A More Affordable California — With Better Everyday Life

San Diego gives you real California — not the high-pressure, high-cost version.

Daily life feels relaxed and student-friendly, especially around:

  • Pacific Beach: surf, sunsets, taco shops
  • Garnet Avenue: cafés, thrift stores, beach cruisers
  • Mission Beach: volleyball courts, cycling paths
  • Liberty Public Market: multicultural food at student-friendly prices
  • Ocean Beach: farmer’s markets + local music
  • La Jolla Shores: calm, scenic weekend beach days

Walking, cycling, and outdoor life define the rhythm of the city — and they cost almost nothing.

San Diego is the most affordable surf city in the USA to study English.

LA vs. San Diego: The Honest Cost Difference

CEL also offers courses in Los Angeles / Santa Monica — an iconic destination with amazing energy.

But here’s what many students discover:

  • LA is more expensive for food, transport, housing, and activities
  • LA means longer commutes
  • LA’s neighborhoods are more spread out
  • LA = amazing for a weekend, not ideal for student budgets

Many choose San Diego as home and visit LA on weekends instead — the best of both worlds, with much lower daily costs.
