Where Students Stay — Feel at Home in Santa Monica

CEL students in Los Angeles can choose between two comfortable housing options designed for independence and cultural exchange:

  • Homestay: Live with a friendly local host family, practice your English daily, and experience real Californian life from the inside. Breakfast or half-board options make it easy to focus on your studies.
  • Shared Apartment: Perfect for students who prefer more independence while still meeting other internationals. Apartments are fully furnished and located within easy reach of both school and the beach.

Both options provide a great mix of comfort, safety, and connection — helping you feel at home from day one.

You can choose between homestays with local families or shared apartments with other students.

Are activities offered?
Yes! Weekly social and cultural events help you discover Los Angeles while practicing English in real-life situations.
