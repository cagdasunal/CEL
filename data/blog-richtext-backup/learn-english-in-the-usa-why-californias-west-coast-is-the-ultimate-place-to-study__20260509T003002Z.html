FAQs about learning English in the USA

Why is the West Coast better for learning English than other regions?
Because it combines outstanding education, multicultural cities, and a year-round sunny climate that boosts motivation and wellbeing.

How long should I study English in the USA?
Most students choose between 4 and 24 weeks, depending on goals. CEL offers flexible schedules for both short and long stays.

Do I need a visa to study English in the USA?
If your course is more than 18 hours per week, you’ll need an F-1 Student Visa. For shorter stays, many students enter with ESTA or a Tourist Visa.

Is accommodation included?
You can choose from various housing options — shared apartments, student houses, or homestays — depending on your preferences and budget.

What level of English do I need to start?
CEL offers courses for all levels, from beginner to advanced. A placement test ensures you’re in the right class.
